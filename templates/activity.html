What is W' balance?

Critical Power (CP) is the highest power you can sustain indefinitely without drawing on your finite anaerobic reserve. W' (W-prime) is that reserve, measured in joules. Every second you ride above CP, W' depletes; every second below CP, it recovers. When W' hits zero you are at your physiological limit — you must slow down.

The recovery model follows Skiba et al. (2012). CP and W' are estimated from your all-time mean-maximal power curve.
