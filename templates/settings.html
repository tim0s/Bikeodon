Mastodon
How to get an access token
  1. Log in to your Mastodon instance and go to Preferences → Development → Your applications.
  2. Click New application. Give it any name (e.g. "Bikeodon").
  3. Under Scopes, make sure write:statuses and write:media are checked (or just enable write).
  4. Click Submit, then click the application name.
  5. Copy the value from Your access token at the bottom of the page and paste it below.
