Strava data export

To import all your Strava activities:

  1. Go to Strava → Settings → My Account → Download or Delete your data
  2. Request your archive and wait for the email
  3. Unzip the archive — activity files are in the activities/ folder (GPX and FIT files)
  4. Select all files in that folder and upload them here
