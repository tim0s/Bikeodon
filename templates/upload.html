Where to get your activity files

Every major platform lets you export activity files. Upload them here — Bikeodon accepts FIT, TCX, and GPX.

Zwift

  1. Go to zwift.com → Profile
  2. Click an activity → Download FIT file

TrainerRoad

  1. Go to trainerroad.com → Calendar
  2. Click a completed workout → Export → Download FIT file

Wahoo ELEMNT

  1. Open the Wahoo app on your phone
  2. Tap an activity → share icon → Export FIT file
  3. Alternatively, connect the ELEMNT to your computer via USB — FIT files are in the ELEMNT/ folder

Garmin Connect

  1. Go to connect.garmin.com → Activities
  2. Click an activity → gear icon → Export Original (FIT format)
  3. To export everything at once: Account → Data Management → Export Your Data

Polar

  1. Go to flow.polar.com → Training → Diary
  2. Click a session → Export session → choose TCX or GPX

Strava bulk export

  1. Go to Settings → My Account → Download or Delete your data
  2. Request your archive and wait for the email
  3. Unzip — activity files are in the activities/ folder
  4. Select all files and upload them here
