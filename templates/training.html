How to import this workout
Zwift (.zwo)

Copy the downloaded .zwo file into Documents/Zwift/Workouts/<your Zwift ID folder>/, then in Zwift go to Workout Mode and pick it from Custom Workouts.

Wahoo (.fit)

Open the Wahoo app, go to the Workouts tab, and use the import/add button to load the downloaded .fit file. It'll sync to your ELEMNT head unit the next time it pairs.

Garmin (.fit)

Connect your Edge or watch via USB and copy the .fit file into the device's GARMIN/NewFiles/ folder (or import it through Garmin Connect's workout import, if your account supports it). It'll appear under Training → Workouts on the device.
